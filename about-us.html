At Pixcel, we believe that discipline starts from young. Getting an early head-start in discipline has a compounded effect. That's why we've gamified discipline, to help students have fun, explore their creativity while getting their work done.

Our Story

We are educators in Singapore, and we spend countless hours with students to help them fulfil their potential. More recently, we've noticed young students seriously struggle to be disciplined with their work. Homework assigned are not completed, and the desire to scroll and play is strong.

We asked ourselves: "What if completing homework could feel as exciting as playing a game?" That's when Pixcel was born - a platform where every completed task helps you build and customize your own virtual room.

We're turning discipline, into a fun and rewarding experience. Today, thousands of students use Pixcel to turn their study sessions into creative building adventures.
